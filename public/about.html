About Me

My name is Joshua Jimenez, and I am an ESL teacher who recently transitioned into the field of web development. Originally from USA, I am currently living and working in South Korea.

I have always been passionate about technology and the internet, and I am thrilled to have made the career switch to web development. I have been studying various coding languages, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. With my knowledge of React, I can create responsive and interactive user interfaces that make websites more dynamic and engaging.

In my free time, I enjoy fixing things around the house, learning about different cultures, traveling, and cooking. I am always looking for new ways to challenge myself and broaden my horizons.

Teaching English as a second language has been a passion of mine for many years, and I have enjoyed helping students improve their language skills and achieve their goals. However, I wanted to explore a new field that combined my passions for technology and teaching, which led me to web development.

Today, I'm proud to call myself a front-end developer who brings a unique perspective to my work. My background in education has taught me the value of clear communication, user-centered design, and accessibility, and I strive to incorporate these principles into everything I create. And while I may have left the classroom behind, I still see myself as a teacher at heart, always seeking to share my knowledge and help others grow.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me. If you have any questions or would like to get in touch, please feel free to contact me.
