Best Movies by Joshua

You'll want to watch these again!

Shawshank Redemption

This movie is about hope, inner hope; a good thing which keeps our lives looking forward.

Coach Carter

Coach Carter is a tale of a coach who practically benched his all star team for not performing academically. It brought out the importance of discipline, respect, teamwork, and humility.

Lean on Me

This film has always struck a chord with my inner-teacher. Young people are and will
continue to be the future and change in our world. Lean On Me shows how young people
can grow and succeed if they're given the opportunity and structure to do so. Now that my
daughter is starting school in a new environment, I find even more reason to focus on guiding,
and fostering her development.
